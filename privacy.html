Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Flora Mate (“App”) is developed and published by individual developer Sergey Hrabrov (referred to as “we”, “our”, “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Data — e‑mail, user name, salted & hashed password.
  • App Content — metadata of plants you create (title, species, next‑watering date). Photos are pre‑defined assets; user uploads are not stored.
  • Analytics & Crash Reports — anonymous usage statistics via Firebase Analytics and crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics.

3. How We Use Data

  • Operate and improve core features (sync, reminders, UI personalisation).
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Monitor stability and performance via aggregated analytics.

4. Legal Basis

We process data under your consent (when you create an account) and for our legitimate interest in maintaining functional, secure services.

5. Sharing With Third Parties

Data is shared only with:

  • Google Firebase – analytics & crash reports (anonymised).

No personal data is sold or disclosed to advertisers.

6. Storage & Security

Data is stored on a secure PostgreSQL instance (Railway, moving to a self‑hosted server in the Russian Federation). Transport is always encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Tokens are kept in iOS Keychain.

7. User Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (delete account).

Contact us (see Section 10) to exercise these rights.

8. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under this age.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy. Changes are posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
